About Daniella

This server has been created as part of the BIO727P Bioinformatics Software Development Group Project in Term 2 of the 2021/22 MSc Bioinformatics intake year.

It's purpose is to retrieve SNP information for specific genomic regions of interest across different populations.

Population-specific SNP information

This includes; name, genomic position, genotype and allele frequencies.

Customizable summary statistics

Including measures of genetic and haplotype diversity, tests against neutrality and population genetic variation.

Graphical display

SNP distribution is plotted in sliding windows across the genomic area of interest for ease of interpretation.
